FAQ

Homepage answers to the first questions serious buyers ask.

Do you only supply zinc steel fence?

No. Zinc steel fence is a current strength, but the site is being built to support broader categories such as temporary fence, welded mesh fence, 358 security fence, and later other common export fence lines.

Can you support custom sizes and project drawings?

Yes. The sales and product flow is designed around project details, so custom height, panel structure, finish, accessory needs, and packaging requirements can be discussed before order confirmation.

Can accessories be supplied together with fence panels?

Yes. Temporary fence in particular should be sold as a system, not only as panels. Bases, clamps, braces, gates, and related accessories can be planned together in the quotation stage.

What kind of buyers is this website written for?

The copy is aimed at importers, distributors, contractors, and project buyers who need practical specification, delivery, and packaging information—not only image-heavy brochure content.

Do you provide application or country-specific guidance?

Yes. Use the application and country pages for buying notes, common specs, and market-specific considerations. We can tailor a recommendation based on your project.
