Foot type selection

20 kg rubber feet are standard for the majority of UK construction and event sites. Concrete ballast feet are recommended for exposed, roadside, or high-wind locations where panel stability is a concern.

Panel gauge selection

3.0 mm infill wire is sufficient for standard construction hoardings, pedestrian exclusion zones, and short-to-medium duration events. Upgrade to 4.0 mm for demolition sites, high-traffic zones, or rental fleet where panels are handled repeatedly.

Finish route

HDG only is the standard for hire fleet and construction hoardings. HDG + powder coat is available in green, black, or white (specify RAL code) for architect-specified or public-facing installations.

Common questions

FAQ — UK temporary fence procurement.

What is the standard panel size for UK hire fleets?

3500×2000 mm is the dominant UK market size. Most UK hire companies and contractors use this dimension as their base stock. Our panels are manufactured to this size as standard, making them directly interchangeable with existing UK hire fleet inventory.

Are your panels CDM 2015 compliant?

CDM 2015 sets site management obligations for principal contractors, not a specific panel standard. Our panels meet the dimensional and structural requirements expected by UK principal contractors. We supply test reports and mill certs to support your site compliance pack.

Do you supply to hire companies or only project buyers?

Both. We regularly supply hire fleet top-ups (500–2000 panels) and single-project site packs. Minimum order is typically one 20ft container. LCL can be arranged for smaller pilot orders.

Can I get privacy mesh at the same time as the panels?

Yes — HDPE knitted debris and privacy mesh in green, black, and white is available as a line item. Specify shade factor (50%, 70%, or 90%) and colour in your RFQ. Mesh is typically sold by the roll but can be cut to panel height.

What is the typical lead time?

Stock items: 15–20 days from order confirmation. Custom spec or large volumes: 30–45 days. Sea freight to UK main ports adds approximately 25–32 days from Chinese port of loading.

Do you provide a landed cost estimate?

Yes — include destination port and incoterms preference in your RFQ and we will return a full landed cost breakdown within 24 hours, including sea freight, insurance, and GB customs duty estimates.
