BS 1722 compliance

BS 1722 compliance — which parts apply to your project.

UK buyers frequently specify BS 1722 for public sector, education, and infrastructure projects. We supply to the applicable parts and can provide test certificates for procurement requirements.

BS 1722 Part 1 — Standard Chain Link

Standard commercial and sports heights. Applies to general-purpose chain link fence for private and public sector applications including schools, sports facilities, and commercial estates.

  • Typical heights: 1.0 m, 1.2 m, 1.5 m, 1.8 m, 2.0 m
  • Post spacing: 3.0 m centres (2.75 m for full compliance)
  • Wire gauge and mesh size must match height specification

BS 1722 Part 10 — Anti-Intruder

Higher security specification with tighter tolerances and enhanced post and tensioning requirements. Required for government, utilities, MOD, and high-risk commercial sites.

  • Minimum height: 2.0 m
  • Anti-climb topping strongly recommended
  • Third-party test certificates available on request

Compliance Documentation

We provide BS 1722 compliance notes, material specifications, and test certificates when required. Specify the applicable part in your RFQ so we can confirm the matching wire gauge, post size, and post spacing.

  • Mill certificates and coating test reports available
  • Third-party (SGS) inspection coordination on request

Shipping & import

Export logistics and UK import process for chain link fence systems.

Chain link fence systems are highly container-efficient — a 20 ft FCL can carry 800–1,200 linear metres depending on height and specification, making full-container orders the most cost-effective route.

UK Main Ports

Felixstowe, Southampton, Tilbury, and Bristol are the primary import gateways for containerised fence systems from China.

  • Felixstowe: largest UK container port, most frequent sailings
  • Southampton: deep-water port, good for large project shipments

Transit Time

20–28 days from China to UK mainland ports. LCL (less-than-container load) adds 5–8 days for consolidation and handling.

  • 20 ft FCL: approx. 800–1,200 linear metres depending on height
  • 40 ft FCL: approx. 2× 20 ft quantity at improved per-unit cost

Packing for Export

Chain link rolls are banded and wrapped on pallets. Posts bundled separately with hardware in cartons. All packing labelled with roll/post count and size for easy customs inspection.

  • Packing list and loading diagram provided before dispatch
  • Stillage packing available for rental fleet returns

UK Customs & Post-Brexit

Full commercial invoice, packing list, and certificate of origin provided for GB customs entry. UKCA and CE marking documentation available on request.

  • Standard UK import duty applies to chain link fence and posts
  • Buyer responsible for duty and VAT unless DAP UK Incoterms agreed
Gates & accessories

Gate and accessory options for chain link fence systems.

Chain link fence systems are rarely quoted as wire rolls alone. Gates, barbed wire toppings, and fixing accessories complete the perimeter and determine the total installation scope.

Swing Gates

Single and double leaf swing gates manufactured to match mesh gauge, post spec, and coating colour. Available as manual or with motor preparation.

  • Single leaf: up to 4.0 m wide
  • Double leaf: up to 12.0 m total width
  • Hinge, latch, drop bolt, and padlock kits bundled

Sliding Gates

Manual and motor-ready sliding gates for vehicle access where swing clearance is limited. Track and roller systems included.

Barbed Wire Toppings

1, 2, or 3 strand barbed wire outrigger arms and tension wire assemblies for higher-security sites. Compatible with HDG and PVC-coated fence systems.

Razor Wire Options

Flat razor wrap and concertina coils available for high-security applications. Typically specified with BS 1722 Part 10 anti-intruder systems.

Anti-Dig & Anti-Lift Fixings

Security-grade foot brackets and anti-dig fence shoes prevent lifting or dig-under attack at the fence base. Standard on utility and high-security specifications.

Posts & Fixing Hardware

Straining posts, line posts, rails, tension bars, post caps, base plates, concrete-in sleeves, and all tie wire and fittings. Colour-matched to mesh where applicable.
