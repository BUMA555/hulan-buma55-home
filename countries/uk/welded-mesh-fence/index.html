Finish & coating

Finish and coating options for UK welded mesh fence systems.

The finish route determines corrosion resistance, appearance, and long-term maintenance cost. UK buyers typically choose based on exposure environment and local authority or project specification requirements.

Hot-Dip Galvanised (HDG)

Long-term outdoor corrosion resistance suitable for exposed coastal and industrial environments. Zinc coating to EN ISO 1461.

  • Best for: utilities, rail, industrial sites, coastal locations
  • Typical zinc coat: 55–85 μm depending on article thickness

Powder Coated (Single Coat)

RAL colour matching to project specification on a pre-galvanised or black steel substrate. EN ISO 12944 performance category C3–C4.

  • Best for: commercial estates, schools, residential developments
  • Popular UK RALs: 6005, 7016, 9005

Duplex (HDG + Powder Coat)

Maximum durability: hot-dip galvanised base with powder coat finish. EN ISO 12944 category C4–C5 for high-value or long-life perimeters.

  • Best for: high-value assets, coastal projects, long-cycle ownership
  • Higher upfront cost, lowest life-cycle maintenance cost

PVC Coated Wire

Polyvinyl chloride coating over galvanised wire for lower-security or aesthetic applications. Available in green, black, and custom colours.

  • Best for: garden, parks, low-security residential boundaries
  • Lower mechanical strength than powder coat

Gate and accessory options that complete the welded mesh fence system.

  • Track and roller systems included
