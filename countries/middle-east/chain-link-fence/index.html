Why GCC Buyers Choose Chain Link

Chain link fence is widely used across the Middle East for construction site perimeters, industrial facilities, sports courts, utility enclosures, and compound boundaries. It is cost-effective, fast to install, and available in coatings proven to withstand UV, salt air, and desert dust over extended project cycles.

  • Construction hoardings and project site perimeters (UAE, KSA, Kuwait, Qatar)
  • Industrial plant and warehouse perimeter fencing
  • Sports courts — football, tennis, padel
  • Airport and utility buffer zones
  • Villa compound and residential boundary fencing

GCC Climate & Corrosion Considerations

GCC environments impose specific demands on fence coating life. Standard electro-galvanised mesh is not adequate for coastal or industrial zones. Our export specification for the Middle East defaults to:

  • HDG wire: minimum 60 g/m² zinc coating for inland sites
  • HDG+PVC duplex: recommended for Jeddah, Abu Dhabi coast, Bahrain, and any site within 5 km of sea
  • UV-stable PVC compound: colour-fast under sustained 45–50°C ambient and high UV index conditions
  • Post wall thickness: minimum 2.0mm for freestanding runs; 2.5mm for sites with shamal wind exposure
