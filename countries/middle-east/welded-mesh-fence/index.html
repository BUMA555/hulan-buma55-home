Where Welded Mesh Is Used in the GCC

Welded mesh panel fence is the standard choice for medium-security and decorative perimeter fencing across the GCC. Its rigid form holds alignment in sandy ground conditions, and the open mesh profile reduces wind load — both important factors in desert and coastal sites.

  • Villa compound and residential community boundaries (UAE, KSA, Qatar)
  • Industrial park and logistics zone perimeters
  • Government and municipal facility enclosures
  • School, hospital, and institutional campuses
  • Utility and telecoms infrastructure enclosures
  • Park, garden, and landscaping boundary definition

Finish Selection for GCC Conditions

Coating choice is critical for the GCC environment. Our recommendations by site type:

  • Inland desert sites (Riyadh, Al Ain, interior KSA): HDG minimum 85 g/m²; powder coat optional for appearance
  • Coastal sites (Dubai waterfront, Jeddah, Manama, Doha): HDG + powder coat duplex; zinc primer under top coat
  • High-visibility residential: RAL 6005 (dark green) or RAL 9005 (black) powder coat over HDG panel
  • Government / municipal: confirm RAL colour with consultant; we can match most standard specifications
