Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about 358 fence for Saudi Arabia projects

What does "358" mean in 358 security fence?

358 fence is named after its approximate imperial mesh dimensions: 3″ × ½″ × 8 gauge. In metric this is 76.2 mm × 12.7 mm aperture with approximately 4 mm wire diameter. The name is used globally as shorthand for anti-climb high-security welded mesh fencing.

Is 358 fence the same as anti-climb fence?

Yes — 358 mesh is the most widely specified form of anti-climb security fence globally. The small 76.2 × 12.7 mm vertical aperture makes it impossible to get a finger or toe hold, defeating most climbing attempts without needing razor wire or electric fencing as a primary topping.

Can 358 fence be cut with bolt cutters?

Standard bolt cutters cannot efficiently cut 4 mm or 5 mm wire. The welded mesh geometry also makes it difficult to create a clean leverage cut. For higher-threat environments, the 5.0 mm heavy-duty specification and close-post spacing provide additional resistance.

What powder coat colours are available for Saudi Arabia?

Any RAL colour is available. The most common specifications for KSA are: RAL 6005 (forest green) for general landscape integration, RAL 7016 (anthracite grey) for modern industrial environments, RAL 9005 (jet black) for high-end residential and government projects, and desert tan/sand colours on request for desert site camouflage.

What is the minimum order quantity for 358 fence panels?

MOQ is 100 panels for standard configurations. Sample orders (1–10 panels) are accepted so buyers can verify quality and specifications before placing full project orders. Sample lead time is typically 5–7 days.

Do you supply installation drawings and technical documentation?

Yes — full CAD installation drawings, panel layout plans, post setting schedules, gate elevation drawings, and material take-off tables are provided with every order. We also supply BS EN 10223-7 compliance test reports and SASO-ready documentation packages.

Can 358 fence be combined with razor wire or electric fencing?

Yes — 358 panels are designed to integrate with a range of toppings: razor wire coil (single or double strand), flat-wrap razor panels, V-arm or Y-arm bracket systems, and electric fence energiser wires. We supply the complete fence system including topping brackets and gate extensions.
