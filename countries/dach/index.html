DACH import FAQ

Common questions from Germany, Austria and Switzerland buyers.

What is the difference between Doppelstabmattenzaun and chain link fence?

Doppelstabmattenzaun is the stronger and more standardised panel route for DACH commercial, industrial, and residential perimeter work. Chain link is still useful for longer runs, sports, agriculture, and lower-security perimeter where installation simplicity and budget control matter more.

Is your fence CE certified and EN 10223 compliant?

We can support CE and EN-route documentation depending on the exact product system and project requirement. The right way to quote DACH work is to mention required standards in the RFQ so the offer, finish route, and documentation package stay aligned from the first round.

What is better for DACH climate: hot dip galvanized or Galfan?

Hot dip galvanized is the dependable baseline for many industrial and commercial fence systems. Galfan is often chosen when buyers want stronger corrosion resistance, especially in industrial exposure or longer-life positioning. Duplex or PVC routes can also be added where appearance and added protection both matter.

Can you export to Germany, Austria and Switzerland with full document support?

Yes. We can prepare the standard export document package and align the quote around your requested commercial route, destination, and compliance notes. If your project has tender wording, send it with the inquiry so the technical reply is shaped correctly.

What is the usual lead time for DACH orders?

Lead time depends on product family, coating route, gate scope, and whether documentation or custom colour is required. For practical quoting, tell us quantity, target market, finish, and required delivery window. We then reply with a shaped production and shipping estimate instead of a generic range.

Can you support distributors and local installers instead of only one-off project buyers?

Yes. This page is intentionally positioned for both distributors and project-led buyers. If you resell locally or install with your own crews, mention your model in the RFQ so we can shape pricing, packing, and repeat-order logic accordingly.

Können Sie auch Tore liefern?

Ja. Schiebetore und Drehtore können als Teil des kompletten Zaunsystems angeboten werden. Geben Sie Öffnungsbreite, Höhe, Bedienart und Zauntyp direkt in der Anfrage an.

Bieten Sie Muster vor der Großbestellung an?

Ja. Sample logic can be discussed for qualifying buyers, especially when colour, finish, or mesh route needs confirmation before a larger order or tender submission.
