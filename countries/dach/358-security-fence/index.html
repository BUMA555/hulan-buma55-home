DACH 358 FAQ

Questions Germany, Austria, and Switzerland buyers usually ask before ordering 358 security fence.

What does 358 mean in 358 security fence?

It refers to the classic anti-climb geometry: 3 inches × 0.5 inch mesh opening and 8 gauge-style wire reference in the traditional naming logic. In metric export quoting, buyers usually focus on 76.2 × 12.7 mm mesh and the actual wire diameter used for the project.

Is 358 the same as Doppelstabmattenzaun?

No. Doppelstabmattenzaun is the stronger standard double-wire panel route for general industrial, commercial, sports, and residential perimeter. 358 is the tighter anti-climb route used when higher-security geometry and restricted-access logic matter more.

Which DACH projects usually choose 358 instead of double wire mesh?

Airports, prisons, substations, wind farms, logistics hubs, government compounds, and some data-centre or utility projects usually move toward 358 because the anti-climb opening, topping compatibility, and security posture are stronger from the start.

Can you support CE and EN documentation requests for DACH 358 inquiries?

Yes. The best approach is to send the exact project wording or tender note early, so the file pack, coating route, and commercial offer stay aligned to your approval process.

Which finish is usually better for Germany, Austria, and Switzerland?

That depends on the site. Standard industrial projects often use pre-galvanized plus powder-coated or hot dip galvanized routes. Harsher exposure or longer-service-life expectations may move the project toward hot dip galvanized, Galfan, or duplex-style logic.

Can the 358 package include gates, arms, and razor wire together?

Yes. That is often the cleanest way to quote a DACH security project, because the post route, arm route, gate opening, and topping should be aligned from the beginning.

Können Sie den 358 Sicherheitszaun auch mit Toren und Auslegern zusammen anbieten?

Ja. Pfosten, Ausleger, Stacheldraht- oder Klingendraht-Vorbereitung, Personen- und Fahrzeugtore sowie passende Befestigungen können als komplettes Sicherheitszaun-Paket angeboten werden.

Welche Informationen machen ein DACH-358-Angebot schneller und genauer?

Land, Stadt, Einsatzort, Zaunhöhe, Drahtstärke, Pfostentyp, Torbedarf, Ausleger oder Drahtaufsatz, Oberflächenbehandlung, Lieferzeit und Dokumentationshinweise. Je klarer diese Punkte sind, desto besser passt das erste Angebot.
