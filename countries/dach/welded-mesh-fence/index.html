What is the difference between 656 and 868 double wire mesh fence?

656 uses 6 / 5 / 6 mm wires and is the standard route for many residential-commercial, school, parking, and lighter industrial projects. 868 uses 8 / 6 / 8 mm wires and is the stronger route for factories, logistics parks, utilities, and buyers who want a heavier-duty panel feel.

Warum ist Doppelstabmattenzaun in Deutschland so gefragt?

Weil das System standardisiert, sauber montierbar, langlebig und für viele Einsatzorte geeignet ist - von Industrie und Logistik bis zu Schulen, Parkplätzen, Sportanlagen und Wohnobjekten. 656 und 868 sind dabei die geläufigsten Spezifikationskürzel.

Which color should we choose for DACH projects?

RAL 6005 moss green, RAL 7016 anthracite grey, and RAL 9005 black are the most common starting points. RAL 7016 is especially strong for modern commercial and residential edges, while RAL 6005 remains common in schools, sports, and municipal environments.

Do DACH buyers usually ask for hot dip galvanized or powder coated mesh?

That depends on the site. Some buyers prioritize appearance and choose powder-coated routes over galvanized wire or HDG base. Others prioritize stronger corrosion performance and move toward hot dip galvanized or Galfan-based routes. The right answer depends on project environment and service-life expectation.

Können Sie passende Tore mit dem Doppelstabmattenzaun zusammen anbieten?

Ja. Schiebe- und Drehtore, Fußgängertore, passende Pfosten, Beschläge und die gleiche Oberflächenbehandlung können als ein gemeinsames System angeboten werden. Das ist für DACH-Projekte meist sauberer als zwei getrennte Angebote.

Can this route work for both distributors and project buyers?

Yes. Distributors often need stock-friendly 656 and 868 assortments with repeat colors, while project buyers need site-specific height, gate, base plate, and finish logic. This page is structured to serve both paths.

What documents should we send with a DACH welded mesh inquiry?

The most useful first message includes target market, panel route, height, finish, color, quantity, gate scope, destination city, and any tender wording or EN-standard references. That lets the first reply match the real DACH RFQ path.

Can Switzerland and Austria use the same panel platform as Germany?

Often yes. The same 656 or 868 platform can usually serve Germany, Austria, and Switzerland, but the finish route, color choice, document quality, and delivery structure may change depending on the buyer and project environment.
