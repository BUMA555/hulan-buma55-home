Applications

Chain link fence applications for USA projects.

Chain link fence covers the widest application range of any fence type. The key selection variables are wire gauge, mesh size, height, and finish — determined by the security level and environment.

Commercial & Industrial Perimeter

Warehouse yards, factory boundaries, logistics parks, and commercial properties. Hot-dip galvanized chain link at 2.1m-3.0m height with 50mm mesh is the standard commercial specification in the US.

  • Best for: warehouse, manufacturing, distribution, commercial real estate
  • Typical spec: BWG 9-11, 50mm mesh, 2.4-3.0m height, hot-dip galvanized
  • Optional: barbed wire topping, razor coil, CCTV integration

Sports Courts & Recreation

Tennis courts, basketball courts, baseball fields, and athletic facilities. PVC-coated chain link in green or black is the US standard for sports court enclosures — matching team colors and providing clean visibility for spectators and cameras.

  • Best for: tennis, basketball, baseball, soccer, multipurpose courts
  • Typical spec: BWG 11, 38-50mm mesh, 3.0-3.6m height, PVC coated (green/black)
  • Optional: windscreen fabric integration, gates, windbreak panels

Schools & Playgrounds

School perimeters, playground enclosures, and recreational areas. Chain link with smaller mesh openings (38mm or 25mm anti-climb) is specified in many US school district standards for child safety and security compliance.

  • Best for: K-12 schools, daycare, community playgrounds, recreational fields
  • Typical spec: BWG 9-11, 38mm mesh, 1.8-2.4m height, hot-dip galvanized
  • Optional: privacy slats, anti-climb mesh, gate access control

Residential Boundaries

Residential property boundaries, backyard enclosures, and pet runs. PVC-coated chain link in green or black maintains appearance in residential neighborhoods while providing a durable, low-maintenance perimeter that outlasts wood fencing in most US climates.

  • Best for: single-family homes, townhome communities, residential developments
  • Typical spec: BWG 11-14, 50mm mesh, 1.2-1.8m height, PVC coated
  • Optional: privacy slats, color-matched posts and gates, pet mesh

Construction Sites

Temporary chain link fence panels for construction site security and safety barrier compliance. OSHA 1926.502(b) specifies temporary fence requirements for construction sites — our temporary chain link panels meet these requirements with stabilizer feet, clamp connections, and galvanized finish.

  • Best for: active construction sites, road work zones, event perimeters
  • Typical spec: BWG 9-10, 50mm mesh, 2.0-2.4m height, hot-dip galvanized panels
  • Optional: windscreen fabric, privacy screening, branded fence covers

Utilities, Infrastructure & Restricted Zones

Utility substations, water treatment plants, telecom sites, and restricted government facilities. Anti-climb chain link with 25mm mesh and barbed wire or razor coil topping is specified for high-security infrastructure applications.

  • Best for: utility substations, water treatment, telecom, transportation infrastructure
  • Typical spec: BWG 6-9, 25-38mm mesh, 2.4-3.6m height, heavy galvanized
  • Optional: razor coil, electronic surveillance integration, crash-rated base

FAQ

Frequently asked questions for USA chain link fence buyers.

What is the difference between electro galvanized and hot-dip galvanized chain link fence?

Electro galvanized (EZ) chain link uses electroplating to apply a thin zinc coating (typically 50-100g/m2). It is cost-effective for dry, indoor, or low-humidity environments. Hot-dip galvanized (HDG) chain link is immersed in molten zinc, producing a coating of 230-275g/m2 minimum — 3x thicker than electro galvanized. For US outdoor and coastal applications, hot-dip galvanized is the standard recommendation. PVC coated over galvanized substrate (duplex system) adds the longest life in coastal and freeze-thaw climates.

What mesh size should I choose for security applications?

For standard commercial perimeter: 50mm (2") mesh with BWG 9-11 wire is the US industry norm. For higher security applications (schools, utilities, restricted sites): 38mm (1.5") mesh with BWG 9 wire reduces handhold and foothold opportunities. For anti-climb requirements (prisons, military, high-security): 25mm (1") mesh or 358 security fence (small square mesh) is recommended. Contact us with your project security level and we will recommend the correct mesh and wire combination.
