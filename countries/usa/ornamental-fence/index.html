FAQ

Questions USA ornamental fence buyers usually ask before they request samples or place the first order.

Should I choose aluminum or steel ornamental fence for my USA project?

Choose aluminum when low maintenance, corrosion resistance, and lighter handling matter most — especially for pool, coastal, and residential work. Choose steel when the project needs stronger structure, heavier gates, wider openings, or more commercial-grade perimeter strength.

Can you match common USA ornamental styles like flat-top, spear-top, and puppy-picket?

Yes. We can quote the most common ornamental profiles used in the USA, including flat-top, spear-top, flush-bottom, pressed spear, and puppy-picket style routes. Share a photo, drawing, or target profile and we will map it into a manufacturable specification.

Can one shipment include panels, posts, gates, and accessories together?

Yes. That is the recommended way to quote ornamental fence for the USA market. Panels only may look cheaper at first, but most revisions happen because posts, brackets, latches, gate hardware, and packing are added too late.

Do you support pool-fence style configurations for US buyers?

Yes. We can configure ornamental fence systems for pool-related projects, but the buyer should provide the actual local barrier and gate requirement. Height, spacing, latch, and self-closing details should follow the applicable local code path.

What finish is best for humid or coastal US markets?

Aluminum with a strong powder-coat route is a common answer for coastal and pool environments. For steel ornamental fence, buyers typically choose galvanized + powder-coated systems and should state the corrosion requirement clearly in the first RFQ.

Can you support distributor stock programs, not just one-off projects?

Yes. Many ornamental fence buyers want repeatable widths, heights, colors, and gate kits for distributor inventory or builder programs. We can quote around a repeatable line card as well as around a specific project drawing.

What is the best first-message format for a USA ornamental fence RFQ?

Send: project type + material route + style + height / width + quantity + gate scope + finish / color + destination port or state + timing + any drawings, reference photos, or approval notes.

Can ornamental fence and other fence categories ship in the same container?

Yes. If the buyer also needs temporary fence, welded mesh, chain link, or another category, it is better to keep them in one RFQ so the loading plan, cost logic, and accessory scope are handled together.
