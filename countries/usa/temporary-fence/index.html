Available Components

Complete Temporary Fence System

Fence Panels

10 × 6 ft hot-dip galvanised panels in standard and heavy-duty gauge. Compatible with all standard US clamp and foot systems.

Rubber Base Feet

Blow-moulded HDPE feet in 20 lb and 40 lb options. Stable on flat surfaces, reusable across multiple deployments.

Steel Base Plates

DOT-approved steel plate feet for highway, bridge and utility corridor projects. Meets state and federal specification requirements.

C-Collar Clamps

Connects panels at 180° and 360° angles. Standard single-bolt and heavy-duty versions available.

Brace Bars

Diagonal brace supports for end-of-run sections and gate corners. Essential for wind-loading compliance on open sites.

Pedestrian & Vehicle Gates

Pedestrian gates: 3 ft (914 mm) clear opening matching panel height. Vehicle gates: 12–16 ft clear opening for truck and equipment access.

Privacy / Debris Mesh

HDPE knitted screen mesh in 50% / 70% / 90% shade factor. Green, black and tan colours to meet local ordinance requirements.

Barbed Wire Arms

18" or 24" arms with 2-strand barbed wire for high-security applications. Bolt-on attachment to panel top rail.

Frequently Asked Questions

USA Buyer FAQ — Temporary Fence Import

What is the standard panel size for US construction sites?

10 ft × 6 ft (3050 × 1830 mm) is the dominant US market size, fully compatible with most US rental companies, contractors and hardware suppliers. Custom sizes available for specification compliance projects.

Are your panels OSHA-compliant?

Yes. Our panels meet OSHA 29 CFR 1926 Subpart M fall protection requirements and ASTM F2611 Standard Specification for Temporary Site Fencing. Mill certs and test reports are provided to support your site safety documentation.

Do you supply to rental companies or only project buyers?

Both. We regularly supply rental fleet top-ups (500–5,000 panels) and single-project site packs. Minimum order is typically one 20 ft container for standard items.

Do you offer Buy America compliant products?

Standard products are manufactured in China for export pricing. Domestic (US-manufactured) content options are available on request for Buy America Act projects — contact us for pricing and lead time.

Can I get DOT-approved panels?

Yes. Steel base plate panels approved for state DOT and federal highway projects are available. Specify the applicable DOT specification in your RFQ and we will confirm compliance.

What is the typical lead time from order to US port?

Stock items: 10–15 days production + 18–35 days sea freight depending on US destination port. Custom specs or large volumes: 25–40 days production. DDP quotes include the full landed cost so you can plan your budget accurately.
