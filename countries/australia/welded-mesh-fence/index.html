Why Welded Mesh Fence for Australia

Welded wire mesh panels offer a cleaner, more rigid perimeter than chain link at a lower cost than steel palisade or ornamental systems. For Australian buyers, sourcing direct from a manufacturer delivers:

  • Consistent weld quality — machine-welded intersections meet pull-out force requirements for security-grade applications
  • Hot-dip galvanized + powder coat — dual-layer corrosion protection suited to Australia's coastal and UV-intense environments
  • AS 1725 compatibility — panel heights and anti-climb profiles aligned to Australian fencing standards for schools, utilities, and government sites
  • Full system supply — posts, rails, gates, and fixings in one order, one shipment
  • Project pricing — direct factory pricing without distributor margin for 500m+ projects

Common Applications in Australia

  • Mining and resources sites — heavy-duty 5.0/4.0mm panels for site access control in WA, QLD, NT
  • Schools and childcare — 1.8m anti-climb profile, powder coat to BCA colour schedule

Finish and Corrosion Protection

Australia's coastal, tropical, and high-UV zones make corrosion protection critical. Standard supply is:

  • Hot-dip galvanizing (HDG) — minimum 85 µm zinc coating on panels and posts, AS/NZS 4680 compliant
  • Polyester powder coat — 60–80 µm DFT over HDG, UV-stable, available in any RAL or Colorbond-matched colour
  • Marine-grade option — 316 stainless steel wire for coastal installations within 500m of salt water

Buyer Notes – Australia

  • Import duty: Welded mesh fence panels typically enter Australia at 0% under FTA provisions (HS 7314.39). Confirm HS classification with your customs broker.
  • Port options: Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Fremantle, Adelaide — we can quote CIF to your preferred port.
  • Lead time: 25–35 days production (colour coat adds 5–7 days) + 18–25 days sea transit to east coast ports.
  • AS 1725 alignment: Specify "AS 1725 compatible" in your RFQ for school, government, or utility applications — we will confirm the applicable height and profile.
  • Colorbond colours: Popular matches include Monument, Basalt, Woodland Grey, and Ironstone — provide colour name or RAL equivalent.
