Why Chain Link Fence for Australia

Chain link remains one of the most widely used fencing systems in Australia for cost-sensitive perimeter, sports, and utility applications. Sourcing direct from a manufacturer delivers:

  • Lower unit cost versus local distributors — especially for large-run commercial or infrastructure projects
  • Full system supply — mesh rolls, line posts, end/corner posts, top rail, tension wire, fittings, and gates in a single shipment
  • Flexible coating options — hot-dip galvanised (HDG), PVC-coated, or duplex HDG+PVC for coastal environments
  • AS 1725 compatible heights — 1.2m, 1.5m, 1.8m, 2.0m, 2.4m, and 3.0m to suit school, utility, and security specifications
  • Sports-spec mesh — 38mm aperture for tennis courts and ball-stop applications

Common Applications in Australia

  • Tennis courts and multi-use sports courts (ball-stop mesh, 3.0m+ height)
  • Construction site hoardings and temporary-to-permanent transitions
  • Industrial yard and warehouse perimeters
  • Water treatment and utility infrastructure enclosures
  • School boundaries and community recreation areas
  • Mining camp and remote site perimeters
  • Residential boundary and driveway entry runs

Coating and Finish Options

  • Hot-Dip Galvanised (HDG): Standard for inland industrial and utility applications — 40–60 µm zinc coating, long service life in dry environments
  • PVC-Coated over galvanised core: Green (RAL 6005), black (RAL 9005), or grey — preferred for sports, schools, and streetscape applications
  • HDG + PVC duplex: Best for coastal Queensland, WA, and NT environments where salt air accelerates corrosion
  • Custom PVC colours: Available on MOQ runs — specify RAL code or Colorbond name

Topping Options

  • Barbed wire — 3-strand or 5-strand on outrigger arms, angled in or out
  • Razor wire flat coil or concertina on topping bracket
  • No topping — standard for schools and sports facilities
  • Extension arms for 45° or vertical barbed wire

Ordering and Lead Times for Australia

  • MOQ: From 500 linear metres for standard HDG stock specs; custom specs from 1,000m
  • Production lead time: 10–15 days for standard HDG; 15–20 days for PVC-coated or custom colour
  • Shipping: FCL to Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Fremantle, or Adelaide — approximately 18–25 days sea transit from east China ports

  • AS 1725 note: Specify "AS 1725 compatible" in your RFQ for school, government, or utility applications — we confirm applicable height and post spacing

Gates and Accessories

  • Barbed wire outrigger arms and tension wire assemblies
