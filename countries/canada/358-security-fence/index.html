Why 358 Security Fence for Canada?

358 fencing — named for its 76.2 mm × 12.7 mm aperture — is the preferred anti-climb, anti-cut barrier for high-security Canadian projects:

  • Anti-climb design — aperture too small for fingers or toes, defeating most scaling attempts
  • Anti-cut resistance — 4.0 mm and 5.0 mm wire diameters resist standard bolt cutters
  • Canadian climate rated — HDG zinc coating plus powder coat duplex withstands freeze-thaw cycles, road salt, and coastal humidity from BC to the Maritimes
  • CSA-compatible procurement — panels and posts supplied with mill certs and test reports suitable for federal and provincial tenders
  • Corrections Canada approved applications — widely used for federal penitentiary perimeters and provincial remand centres
