Why Welded Mesh Fence for Canada

Welded wire mesh panels deliver a cleaner, more rigid perimeter than chain link at a lower cost than steel palisade or ornamental systems. For Canadian buyers, the key advantages of sourcing from a manufacturer are:

  • Consistent weld quality — machine-welded intersections with tensile strength specs available on request
  • V-beam anti-climb geometry — horizontal V-fold stiffeners eliminate panel flex and make climbing harder
  • Full system supply — panels, concrete-in posts, anti-lift brackets, tension wire, and personnel gates in one order
  • Powder coat colour range — RAL 6005 (dark green), RAL 7016 (anthracite), RAL 9005 (black), and custom colours
  • Galvanized-before-weld option — individual wires galvanized before welding for superior corrosion protection at welds

Canadian Market Notes

  • Powder coat vs galvanize: Most Canadian specifiers require both — galvanized base with powder coat finish. We supply dual-finish as standard on exported panels.
  • Cannabis facility specs: Health Canada licensed producer perimeter requirements typically call for 1.8m–2.4m anti-climb with CCTV compatibility. Our standard anti-climb aperture (200×50mm) meets this profile.
  • Winter performance: Powder coat adhesion on galvanized substrate handles Canadian temperature cycling (-40°C to +40°C) without delamination when applied to correct DFT spec.
  • Import duty: Welded mesh fence panels typically enter Canada at 0% MFN under HS 7314.39. Confirm HS classification with your customs broker.
  • Lead time: 30–45 days production (colour coat adds time) + 18–22 days sea transit to Vancouver or Montreal.
