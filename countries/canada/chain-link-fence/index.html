Why Chain Link Fence?

Chain link fence remains Canada's most popular utilitarian fencing option for good reasons:

  • Cost-effective — lowest cost-per-metre of any permanent fence type
  • Durable — galvanized or vinyl-coated options resist rust and corrosion
  • Low maintenance — no painting, staining, or sealing required
  • Quick installation — faster to install than wood or vinyl panels
  • Secure — effective boundary marker and deterrent
  • Transparent — maintains sightlines for safety and surveillance

Popular Applications in Canada

  • Residential yards, dog runs, and garden enclosures
  • Commercial property perimeters and loading areas
  • Industrial sites, warehouses, and storage yards
  • Sports fields, playgrounds, and recreational areas
  • Municipal parks and public facility boundaries

Key Buying Considerations for Canadian Buyers

  • Climate rating — ensure materials are rated for freeze-thaw cycles in your region
  • Post setting — concrete-set posts are standard; in sandy or soft soil, longer posts may be needed
  • Coating — PVC/vinyl coating adds ~30% cost but significantly extends lifespan near coastal areas (BC, Maritimes)
  • Gate hardware — plan for heavy-duty gate hinges and latches in high-wind areas
  • By-law compliance — check municipal fence height and placement bylaws before purchasing
